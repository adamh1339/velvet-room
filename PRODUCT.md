# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Persona RPG series fans (P3 Reload, P4 Golden, P5 Royal) ranging from first-time players to veterans. They use this mid-session — stuck on a boss, planning a fusion build, choosing a next game — on desktop or phone, often in a dim room with the game running nearby. Primary task: get a fast, accurate Persona strategy answer without leaving their session.

## Product Purpose

Velvet Room is a fine-tuned AI strategy guide for the Persona series. It answers questions about fusions, boss strategies, Confidants, dungeon progression, and game recommendations. A secondary fusion calculator computes exact fusion results from structured game data. Success means the user gets the right answer faster than googling, with no hallucinated mechanics.

## Brand Personality

Arcane, precise, cinematic. The Velvet Room in the games is a liminal space — between dream and reality, outside time — staffed by enigmatic attendants who speak in measured tones. The interface should feel like entering that space: quiet authority, deep blue-black atmosphere, no noise.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI chat UIs (ChatGPT, Claude.ai clones) — white backgrounds, floating avatars, timestamp clusters
- Colorful gaming apps (Discord, Twitch, game companion apps) — neon accents, busy chrome, achievement badges
- SaaS dashboard patterns — sidebar nav, data tables, metric cards, topbar breadcrumbs
- Glassmorphism used decoratively — blurs everywhere, frosted panels as aesthetic filler

## Design Principles

1. **The room, not the tool.** The interface is the Velvet Room, not an app. It should feel like a place with atmosphere, not a product with features.
2. **Silence is the default.** Minimum chrome. No labels for labeled things, no borders for bordered things. Every visible element justifies its presence.
3. **Precision over personality.** The AI is accurate and measured, not chatty. The UI reflects that: tight copy, controlled color, deliberate motion.
4. **Mode color is the only brightness.** The game-mode accent color is the only warm or saturated element. Everything else is near-black or near-invisible.
5. **Motion reveals, never decorates.** Animations signal state changes (message arriving, loading) and nothing else. No idle loops, no hero animations.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum for text contrast. Respect prefers-reduced-motion. No information conveyed by color alone.
